Fried Chicken

Fried Chicken is one of my favorite food of all time.

As simple, as easy, as tasty as it gets! With this classic fried chicken recipe, you can't go wrong - so go right and fry up some chicken tonight!

Ingredients

  • 1 (4lb) chicken cut into pieces
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 quarts of vegetable oil for frying

instructions

  1. take pieces of chicken and salte and pepper to taste
  2. roll the chicken pieces into the flour
  3. take a large skillet and heat the 2 quarts of vegetable oil in medium heat
  4. fry chicken pieces on medium heat until golden brown in all sides
